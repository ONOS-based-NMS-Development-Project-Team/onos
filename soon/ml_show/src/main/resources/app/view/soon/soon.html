MS_RDI

Alarm explanation

The MS_RDI alarm indicates that the last three digits of K2 in the signal received by the line are "110", and the remote multiplex section reception fails.

Alarm attributes

Alarm level Alarm type

secondary

communication

Alarm parameters

none

Impact on the system

If this alarm is generated, it indicates that the last three digits of K2 in the signal received on the optical port of the circuit board are “110”. unavailable.

Possible Causes

The possible reasons for the alarm MS_RDI are as follows:

  • The peer station received the R_LOS/R_LOC/R_LOF/MS_AIS/B2_EXC alarm.

  • The receiving part of the peer station is faulty.
  • This site sends a partial failure.
